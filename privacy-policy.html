Summary: Custom New Tab Manager is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All customization settings are stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

Custom New Tab Manager extension respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. Here's what we do and don't collect:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Browsing history or website visits
  • Search queries or typed URLs
  • Personal files or documents
  • Location data
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

Information We DO Collect/Store (Locally Only):

  • Your customization preferences (theme colors, layout settings)
  • Quick access links you choose to add
  • Display preferences (time format, language)

Important: All data is stored locally in your browser's storage and never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. How We Use Information

The minimal data we store locally is used solely to:

  • Provide you with a personalized new tab experience
  • Remember your customization settings between browser sessions
  • Display your preferred quick access links
  • Maintain your chosen theme and layout preferences

We do not use this information for advertising, analytics, or any commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage:

All extension data is stored using Chrome's local storage API, which means:

  • Data remains on your device only
  • Data is encrypted by your browser
  • Data is not accessible to other websites or extensions
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Data Security:

  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No tracking cookies or beacons
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • Extension code is open-source and auditable

4. Extension Permissions

Our extension requests minimal permissions:

Storage Permission:

Purpose: To save your customization preferences locally on your device.

Scope: Limited to extension-specific data only.

Access: No access to other websites' data or your browsing information.

New Tab Override:

Purpose: To replace the default new tab page with our custom interface.

Scope: Only affects new tab pages, not existing tabs or websites.

5. Third-Party Services

Custom New Tab Manager does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Data collection services
  • Remote servers or APIs

Quick Links: When you click on quick links (Google, YouTube, etc.), you'll be redirected to those websites, which have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the extension settings
  • Modification: You can change or update any settings at any time
  • Deletion: You can reset all settings or uninstall the extension to remove all data
  • Portability: Settings are standard and can be manually backed up if needed

Data Deletion:

To completely remove all extension data:

  1. Go to Chrome Settings > Extensions
  2. Find "Custom New Tab Manager"
  3. Click "Remove" to uninstall
  4. All local data will be permanently deleted

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we'll provide notice through the extension
  • Your continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Previous versions will be archived and available upon request
